About Us

Meet Josh Nielsen, PhD - Your Automation Partner

Hi, I'm Josh Nielsen, PhD in Industrial and Systems Engineering. I build tools that eliminate the most tedious and error-prone parts of business workflows so you can focus on what you do best.

My Philosophy: Listen, Validate, Deliver

I believe the best solutions come from truly understanding your needs. My approach is built on three core principles:

  • Listen First: I take the time to understand your workflow, pain points, and goals before proposing any solution
  • Validate Everything: Every solution is tested and validated by domain experts to ensure it's practical and useful
  • Carefully Defined Solutions: I focus on reliability and precision, ensuring every automation works exactly as intended

Academic Excellence

I received my PhD in Industrial and Systems Engineering from the University of Louisville, where I was awarded the John M. Houchens Prize for Outstanding Dissertation - the highest honor given to the most meritorious doctoral dissertation across all fields in my graduating class. This is the field that invented Lean Manufacturing and Six Sigma - the gold standards for business process improvement that every business leader knows.

My research focused on applying these proven engineering principles to solve complex healthcare challenges, demonstrating my ability to tackle real-world problems with innovative solutions. When I say I can optimize your workflow, I'm speaking from the discipline that literally wrote the book on process improvement.

For architects in particular, I understand that your time is valuable and your work is complex. I don't just build tools - I build solutions that fit seamlessly into your existing workflow, eliminating the repetitive tasks that slow you down while preserving the creative aspects you love.
