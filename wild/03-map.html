Where do you want to look?

The scenarios in WILD are grouped into five areas. Pick an area to read the stories inside it. The map will be here whenever you want to come back.

Recipient relationship

Stories about who the recipient is, the relationship between donor and recipient, and what each has said to the other about the situation.

16 stories · 6 sub-areas

The conversation around you

Stories about the people and voices around you when donation is on the table, including family expectations, possible coercion, and social media.

5 stories · 4 sub-areas

Medical eligibility

Stories about who can donate, what medical and lifestyle requirements have to be met, and what activities may have to change after donation.

2 stories · 3 sub-areas

Logistical problems

Stories about the practical questions a willing donor faces, including lost wages, travel, caregiving, and the pace of evaluation.

4 stories · 3 sub-areas

Life on one kidney

Stories about what life looks like after donation, including long-term health, meeting the recipient years later, and what happens when a transplant fails or follow-up ends.

6 stories · 3 sub-areas
