AutoTagger

Revit Door & Room Tagging Automation

A real-world example of how automation transforms architectural workflows

The Challenge

Architects designing multi-unit buildings (apartments, hotels, etc.) spend hours manually tagging doors and rooms in Revit. This repetitive task is:

  • Time-consuming (requires extensive renumbering after every client change)
  • Error-prone (manual mistakes cause expensive revisions)
  • Boring and demotivating for skilled professionals
  • Not scalable as project complexity increases

Large multi-unit projects can have hundreds of rooms requiring consistent tagging

Each floor adds complexity to the tagging and coordination process

The Solution

I developed a custom pyRevit tool that automatically tags hundreds of doors and rooms in seconds, with perfect accuracy. The tool:

  • Analyzes room layouts and door positions
  • Handles complex shapes (T-shapes, L-shapes, U-shapes)
  • Applies cascading logic for inner rooms (bedrooms, bathrooms, etc.)
  • Integrates seamlessly with existing Revit workflows
  • Works with Revit 2020 and newer versions
